Why use GWizz?


Gmail is possibly the world’s greatest email service provider and offers exceptional delivery and inbox open rates when used correctly. GWizz has made it even easier for you to capitalize on these benefits.

GWizz is web-based, which means you have nothing to install, you don’t have to set up complex mailing servers, configure DNS records or SMTP settings. Just simply create a GWizz account account and add all your Gmail accounts.

The sending limits when sending emails from Gmails web interface is 500 emails per day with their free email accounts or 2000 per day when using Google workspace accounts.

GWizz uses Google’s SMTP servers to send mail, this is legitimate and allowed by Google subject to not sending SPAM. However, they have lower limits than when sending via Google's web interface. Typically this is around 60-70 emails per day using a free Gmail account and around 300 per day using a Google Workspace account. Gwizz allows you to add unlimited Gmail accounts which inturn offers unlimited email sending capabilities.

With GWizz you can assign unique IP addresses to individual Gmail accounts or a Group of accounts if you’re using Google Workspace. This way if a spammer should use GWizz to attempt spamming people their actions will have no effect on your accounts.

GWizz is extremely user friendly and offers many benefits to ensure the emails you send achieve high open rates.

Benefits include scheduling emails to be sent during operating hours set by you. Subject lines can be personalized to include the recipient's first name or other relevant information. You can add multiple subject lines so no two emails go out looking exactly the same.

When you write your copy GWizz will warn you if it includes potential spam triggers allowing you to easily switch the content. It’s important that you do not send emails that bounce. GWizz allows you to plug in ‘Million Verifier’ to validate your emails just prior to sending.

Included in this powerful one of a kind software tool is a powerful import function allowing you to filter the email address you import. It removes duplicates and email addresses that have previously unsubscribed. But it also allows you to include or exclude certain domain extensions, role based emails and free email accounts saving you endless hours managing your data.

When setting up a campaign you can use multiple lists you have added and send the email using multiple gmail accounts you have added.
